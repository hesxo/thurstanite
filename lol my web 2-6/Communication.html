Communication

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) has transformed the way we communicate, allowing us to connect with people and information in new and innovative ways. This essay will explore the various uses of ICT in communication and the impact it has had on our personal and professional lives. One of the most significant ways in which ICT is used in communication is through social media platforms. Social media has become an integral part of our daily lives, allowing us to connect with people from all over the world, share information, and build relationships. Platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and LinkedIn have changed the way we interact with others, providing a platform for people to share their thoughts, ideas, and experiences with others.
